ABOUT

HIDDENX

HiddenX is an engaging Cybersecurity CTF event set to take place on , 2024, from 4:30 to 6:30 P.M. at PSG College of Technology. With an anticipated participation of 50 students across all departments and years, this event will immerse participants in the intricate worlds of social engineering and cryptography. Organized by a dedicated team, HiddenX introduces a unique team dynamic where participants are secretly assigned to either Team X or Team Z, fostering an atmosphere of suspense and strategy. Participants will tackle various challenges, including cryptography puzzles and social engineering tasks, while navigating deception and misinformation tactics to uncover hidden complexities. The scoring system is designed to maintain intrigue, revealing only team scores throughout the competition, culminating in a final reveal of individual performance and team rankings. By fostering collaboration and emphasizing the importance of trust and misdirection, HiddenX promises to be an educational and thrilling experience for all involved.
